KernelExpl0it's Top Trusted

There are many good sources, but these we trust the most.

MODDED WARFARE

From the PS3 to PS5, Nintendo Switch, and Xbox 360, MODDED WARFARE offers comprehensive guides for modding and homebrew. Known for keeping users informed on the latest updates, they're a highly trusted source for console modding and news.

MrMario2011

MrMario2011 is a trusted source for console modding and homebrew. They offer a wide range of tutorials and guides for various consoles, including the PS2, PS3, PSVita, Xbox 360, and more. MrMario2011 is known for their detailed and easy-to-follow tutorials, as well as awesome modchats.

Macho Nacho Productions

Macho Nacho Productions creates videos on retro consoles, focusing on restoration, modding, and upgrades. Their content is perfect for enthusiasts looking to bring new life to classic systems with detailed guides and insights.
